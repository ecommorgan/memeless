When you use the Memeless website or app (the “Service”), we and our partners may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and ensure the proper functioning of our Service.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit websites or use applications. They allow us to remember your preferences, recognize your device, and improve how the Service performs.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of purposes, including:

  • Ensuring essential site functionality, such as login and security
  • Remembering user preferences and customization settings
  • Analyzing traffic and improving site performance
  • Delivering relevant content or advertisements
  • Supporting customer support and troubleshooting

4. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for basic operation and security. Without these, the site may not function properly.
  • Functionality Cookies: Allow the Service to remember choices such as theme, language, and region.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information on how visitors use our Service, such as which pages are most visited.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by third parties to deliver personalized ads or measure campaign effectiveness.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies or receive alerts before they are placed. However, disabling some cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.

You can also use online resources like AboutCookies.org or YourOnlineChoices.com for more information on managing cookies.
